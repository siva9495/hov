# Privacy Policy for Home of Veltech

**Last updated:** 2023-11-20

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled by the Home of Veltech Android application ("the App").

## 1. Information We Collect

The App collects and stores the following information on your device:

*   **Login Credentials:** Your username and password for the `ams.veltech.edu.in` student portal.
*   **Student Profile:** Your name and academic branch.
*   **Academic Data:** Your attendance records, semester results (including grades and CGPA), and class timetable.

This information is sourced directly from your student portal.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to provide the core functionality of the App, which includes:

*   Displaying your attendance, results, and timetable.
*   Personalizing your in-app experience.
*   Providing offline access to your academic data.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

*   **Local Storage:** All collected data is stored exclusively on your device. No data is transmitted to or stored on any third-party servers.
*   **Encryption:** Your login credentials and other sensitive information are stored in an encrypted format using Android's `EncryptedSharedPreferences` to protect it from unauthorized access.
*   **Secure Communication:** The App communicates with the `ams.veltech.edu.in` portal over a secure HTTPS connection, ensuring that all data transmitted between your device and the portal is encrypted.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. All data remains on your device and is used only for the purposes described in this policy.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App and you will be notified of any significant updates.
